Frequently Asked Questions

How does the recycling app work?

This app helps you identify recyclable items and locate nearby recycling centers.

What types of materials can I recycle?

You can recycle plastics, glass, metals, paper, and more. Specific materials accepted may vary by location.

How can I find the nearest recycling center?

Use the app's map feature to locate recycling centers based on your current location or search by address.

Is there a fee to use the app?

No, the app is completely free for users. Our goal is to encourage recycling and sustainability.
